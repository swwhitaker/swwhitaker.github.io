Landscape and Travel Photography

Welcome!

Hi! Welcome to my as yet very web 1.0 looking page about landscape and travel photography! I have created this page as an assignment to learn more about html and to play with creating content, so it will be getting better as I progress. The twin foci of this page is landscape and travel photography because they are the two kinds of photography I enjoy doing, and because they share a sense of trying to translate the feeling of being in a remarkable setting into an image that can be a touchstone of memory for me and can communicate as much as possible about what makes that setting remarkable to others.

You can get an idea of the kind of images I like to create in the slideshow below. You can see even more on my online gallery.

One of My Favorite Landscape Photography Pages

One of my favorite landscape photography pages and a good source of inspiration is Joshua Cripps Photography.

I have been enjoying this page for years for several reasons:
  • The quality of the portfolio is stellar. I really enjoy viewing the lanscape photography galleries.
  • The video courses and tips and tutorials pages are a great mix of free introductory content as well as useful paid content to guide other burgeoning landscape photographers in important aspects of the craft like processing, field work, equipment, composition.
  • It includes a great blog with sharable content that I see on social media and it keeps me coming back.
  • Joshua has put out some great ebooks, and offers samples gratis.
